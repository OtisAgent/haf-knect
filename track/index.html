Location sharing — terms & privacy

What we record: your device’s location throughout the delivery — a journey trail, with accuracy — captured while this screen is open and sharing is on. This happens only while you are on an active HAF job, never in your own time.

Live sharing: your current position is shown to the customer/business for this job so they can follow it. You appear by your driver name — your phone number is never shown.

Internal use & retention: HAF keeps the journey trail to improve routing, ETAs, relay planning and the wider network — used internally, and in aggregated form where possible. It is not sold, and not shared with anyone beyond this job’s customer. Trails are kept for up to 12 months, then deleted.

Your control & rights: sharing is voluntary — tap Stop or close this page at any time to end it; nothing is recorded while it is off. You can request a copy of your data, or have it erased, at any time.

Data controller: HAF Logistics Ltd (useHAF). Lawful basis: carrying out your delivery work and HAF’s legitimate interest in running and improving the network. Questions, access or erasure: privacy@usehaf.co.uk. By tapping Start you confirm you have read and agree to these terms.
